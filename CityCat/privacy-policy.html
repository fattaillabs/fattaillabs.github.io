Privacy Policy

Effective: July 21, 2026 · FatTail Labs

CityCat ("the app") is developed by FatTail Labs. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

1. What we collect

  • Device identifier — a per-device ID (Apple's identifierForVendor) used to keep your coin balance and map uploads on our server. It is not linked to your name, email, or any account — CityCat has no user accounts.
  • Uploaded map videos — if you choose to upload a street video as a game map, the video (re-encoded, audio removed), a thumbnail, a title you enter, and your device's region (country code) are stored on our servers (Cloudflare R2, USA/global). Uploads are reviewed before becoming public.
  • Purchase records — coin pack purchases are processed by Apple. We receive only an anonymous transaction identifier to credit coins; we never see payment details.
  • Advertising data — the app shows ads via Google AdMob, which may collect the advertising identifier (IDFA, subject to your App Tracking Transparency choice), IP address, and device information to serve and measure ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.

2. What we do NOT collect

  • No accounts, names, emails, or contact lists.
  • Camera frames in Live Mode are processed entirely on your device for vehicle detection and are never stored or transmitted.
  • Videos you play from your photo library stay on your device unless you explicitly upload them as maps.

3. Uploaded maps — your choices and our rules

  • Uploading is entirely optional and clearly labeled.
  • Videos with identifiable faces or readable license plates are rejected during review.
  • Approved maps are public: any CityCat player can download and play them.
  • To request removal of a map you uploaded, contact us with the map title — we will remove it and its stored files.

4. Data retention

Coin ledger entries and map metadata are retained while the service operates. Rejected map videos are deleted at rejection. You may request deletion of all data tied to your device ID at any time.

5. Children

CityCat is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
